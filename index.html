Basic HTML Animals

This page introduces a few interesting animals and how they relate to different parts of web design.

The Llama

The Llama loves anything organized. Whenever she sees a list, she walks over calmly and inspects each item with care, as if evaluating its usefulness.

The Anaconda

The Anaconda is fast and sneaky. He uses anchor tags to silently move through the page without being noticed until it’s too late.

Advanced HTML Animals

Llama
A tall, furry animal with a serious stare. Known for its love of structure and dislike of cluttered layouts.
Anaconda
A long, quiet snake that uses hyperlinks to move quickly and silently between sections of the page.
Hippopotamus
Large and mysterious. No one’s really sure what he does, but he’s definitely part of the group.

Emphasis and importance

Two things that matter a lot to me are consistency and progress. I try to stay focused on small wins every day because even if things feel slow, moving forward still counts.
